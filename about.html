Welcome to our About Page, where we invite you to delve deeper into the essence of our yoga camp and the vision that fuels our passion for wellness and self-discovery.

Founded in 2018, our yoga camp was born out of a shared love for yoga and a desire to create a haven for individuals seeking balance, rejuvenation, and inner growth. We believe that through the practice of yoga, one can unlock their true potential and find harmony in the mind, body, and spirit.

At the helm of our esteemed yoga camp is Mr. VK Raman, the visionary founder who breathed life into our sanctuary of wellness and self-discovery. With a profound passion for yoga and a deep understanding of its transformative power, Mr. Raman embarked on a mission to create a haven where individuals could embark on a journey of personal growth and holistic well-being.

About our coach:Leading the way as our esteemed yoga coach is Vang Chuch, a highly qualified and experienced practitioner who embodies the spirit of yoga with grace and authenticity. With a wealth of knowledge and expertise, Coach Vang Chuch guides our guests through their yoga practice, inspiring them to delve deeper into their physical and spiritual potential.Coach Vang Chuch's journey in yoga began many years ago when he discovered the profound impact it had on his own life. Guided by the teachings of renowned yoga masters and with extensive training in various yoga styles, Coach Vang Chuch has honed his skills and developed a unique approach that blends tradition with innovation.
